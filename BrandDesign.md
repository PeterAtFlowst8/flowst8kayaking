# FlowSt8 Kayaking - Brand Design Prompt

You are a brand designer creating a complete visual identity for **FlowSt8 Kayaking**, a European kayak coaching business.

## Brand Name & Digital Presence
- **Name:** FlowSt8 Kayaking (the "8" is intentional - represents infinity/endless flow)
- **Website:** flowst8kayaking.eu
- **Instagram:** @flowst8kayaking
- **Tagline:** "Find Your FlowSt8" or "Master the Water. Master Your Mind."

## Brand Personality
- **Confident** - Expert coaching, trustworthy
- **Energizing** - Adventure and excitement
- **Mindful** - Flow state philosophy (skill meets serenity)
- **Encouraging** - Supportive, accessible to all levels
- **Authentic** - Real outdoor experience, European-based

## Design Inspiration
Reference the attached brand examples:
1. **Otter** - Playful speech bubbles, bold shapes, friendly wordplay
2. **Hurry Up & Have Fun** - Bold typography, vibrant color blocking, high energy
3. **EyeDrop** - Clean/minimal, smooth gradients, professional modern aesthetic

**Direction:** Blend adventure energy with zen mindfulness. Bold but not aggressive. Professional but approachable.

## Visual Identity Requirements

### Logo Concepts
- Incorporate the "8" as a design element (can suggest infinity ∞)
- 3 variations needed: full logo, icon only, horizontal
- Should work in water/outdoor contexts
- Consider: wave patterns, figure-8 paddle motion, infinity symbol integration

### Color Palette
**Primary Colors:**
- Deep Ocean Blue: #0B3D91 or #1E4D7B
- Turquoise/Teal: #00A99D or #40E0D0
- White: #FFFFFF

**Accent Colors:**
- Sunset Orange: #FF6B35 or #FF8C00 (safety, energy)
- Forest/Seafoam Green: #2D5016 or #3EB489

**Usage:** 60% blues/white, 30% teal, 10% orange accents
**Special:** The "8" in FlowSt8 could be the accent color

### Typography
**Recommended Pairings:**
- **Headings:** Montserrat Bold, Outfit Bold, or Bebas Neue
- **Body:** Open Sans, Nunito Sans, or Inter
- Numbers (especially the "8") must integrate naturally

**Hierarchy:**
- H1: 48-60px | H2: 36-42px | H3: 24-28px | Body: 16-18px
- Line height: 1.5-1.6 | Letter spacing: +0.5-1px on headings

### Imagery Style
- Dynamic action shots (kayaker in rapids, splashing water)
- Natural lighting (golden hour, bright daylight)
- Vibrant colors (orange kayaks, blue water, green forests)
- Mix of first-person perspective and environmental wide shots
- Cartoon/playful illustrations for educational content (like kayak reference image)

### Graphic Elements
- Wave patterns and water textures
- Speech bubbles for social media (Otter-style)
- Color-blocked sections (Hurry Up-style)
- The number 8 and infinity symbol ∞ as recurring motifs
- Clean whitespace (EyeDrop-style)

## Deliverables Needed

### 1. Logo Package
- Primary logo (full lockup)
- Secondary logo (horizontal)
- Icon/symbol only (for social profile)
- Minimum size specs and clear space
- Light and dark versions

### 2. Brand Style Guide
- Color palette (HEX, RGB values)
- Typography system with font hierarchy
- Logo usage rules (do's and don'ts)
- Graphic element examples

### 3. Website Design Mockup (flowst8kayaking.eu)
**Homepage sections:**
- Hero: Bold headline + kayaking background + CTA buttons
- Services: 3 color-blocked program tiers (beginner/intermediate/advanced)
- About: Coach story + credentials
- Testimonials: Speech bubble style
- Instagram feed integration
- Footer: Simple, clean contact info

**Inspiration:** Combine Hurry Up's color blocking + EyeDrop's clean layout

### 4. Social Media Templates (Instagram)
- Profile picture (logo icon)
- Post templates:
  - Tip graphics (bold color backgrounds)
  - Quote posts (water texture backgrounds)
  - Announcement posts (speech bubble style)
- Story highlight covers
- Grid layout suggestion (9-post pattern)

### 5. Business Card Design
- Front: Logo + name + title
- Back: Water texture + contact info (email, phone, website, location)

## Voice & Messaging
- **Writing style:** Casual but knowledgeable, encouraging not intimidating
- **Key phrases:** "Find your flow," "Navigate with confidence," "From beginner to bold"
- **Avoid:** Overly technical jargon, fear-based messaging, corporate speak
- **Context:** European market (slightly more formal than US, values credentials and safety)

## Content Themes (The 8 Pillars)
1. Technique tutorials
2. Flow state/mindfulness
3. Location spotlights
4. Student wins
5. Safety & gear
6. Motivation
7. Behind the scenes
8. Community/events

## Brand Applications to Show
- Website homepage mockup
- Instagram profile + 3-6 post examples
- Logo on kayaking gear/apparel mockup
- Email header template
- Business card

## Design Goals
✓ Memorable and distinctive (the "8" makes it unique)
✓ Works across digital and physical applications
✓ Appeals to beginners AND advanced kayakers
✓ Reflects both adventure excitement and mindful flow state
✓ Professional enough for corporate team-building, approachable enough for hobbyists
✓ Strong visual identity that stands out in outdoor/adventure space

Create a cohesive brand identity that captures the energy of whitewater kayaking and the zen of flow state, with the infinity concept (∞/8) woven throughout.
